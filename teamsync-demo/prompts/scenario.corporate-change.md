Scenario module: Common corporate environment

Context:
A professional services company is experiencing missed handoffs, uneven accountability, and delayed decisions during cross-functional delivery. Leadership wants a practical simulation before changing operating cadence company-wide.

Use case for demo:
- Walk stakeholders through a safe, realistic rehearsal.
- Show how role strengths can reduce conflict and increase throughput.
- Demonstrate how TeamSync handles workflow changes without restarting the process.

Facilitator objective:
Run one full simulation cycle across discovery, alignment, execution sprint, and retrospective.

Realistic friction injects:
- A high-priority client escalates a timeline concern in week 2.
- One leader requests an urgent workflow shortcut that bypasses approvals.
- Team clarity score drops below target after ownership reshuffle.

Expected simulation outcomes:
- A revised handoff map with explicit owners.
- A meeting cadence that balances speed with decision quality.
- A shortlist of workflow experiments with measurable success metrics.
